Mounjaro FAQs

Common questions about Mounjaro

Clinical trials show patients lose up to 22.5% of their body weight over 72 weeks. Most patients lose 2–4 stone within 6–12 months. Individual results vary based on starting weight, adherence, and lifestyle factors.
Mounjaro comes in a pre-filled pen that's easy to use. You inject it once weekly under the skin of your stomach, thigh, or upper arm. The needle is very small and most patients report minimal discomfort. We provide full instructions and video guides with your first delivery.
The most common side effects are nausea, diarrhoea, reduced appetite, vomiting, and constipation. These are usually mild and improve over time as your body adjusts. Starting with a low dose and gradually increasing helps minimise side effects.
Most patients take Mounjaro for 6–12 months to reach their weight loss goals. Some continue longer for maintenance. Your prescriber will work with you to determine the right duration based on your progress and goals.
Mounjaro is suitable for adults with a BMI of 30+ (or 27+ with weight-related health conditions). It's not suitable if you're pregnant, breastfeeding, or have certain medical conditions. Complete our online assessment and our prescribers will determine if Mounjaro is right for you.
