Treatment FAQs

The choice depends on your weight loss goals, medical history, and personal preferences. Mounjaro typically offers higher average weight loss compared to Wegovy. Our prescribers will review your assessment and recommend the most suitable option based on your individual circumstances.

The key differences are:

  • Active ingredient: Mounjaro contains Tirzepatide (dual GLP-1 and GIP agonist), Wegovy contains Semaglutide (GLP-1 agonist only)
  • Weight loss potential: Mounjaro up to 22.5% vs Wegovy's 20.7% body weight reduction
  • Dosing schedule: Both once-weekly injections but different titration schedules
  • Licensing: Mounjaro for type 2 diabetes and weight management; Wegovy specifically for weight management
Yes, both Mounjaro and Wegovy are clinically proven and approved by the FDA and MHRA. Common side effects including nausea and reduced appetite are typically mild and improve over time. Our prescribers carefully assess your medical history to ensure the treatment is safe and appropriate for you.
