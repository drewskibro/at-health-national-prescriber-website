Why We Exist

Our Story

AT Health was founded in 2021 with one clear mission — to make prescription weight loss treatments accessible, affordable, and effective for everyone.

1

Experienced Medical Roots

Operated by Prescription Point Limited, our team brings over a decade of experience in the medical sector. We've grown from a simple idea into a trusted service supporting over 10,000 patients across the UK.

2

Solving the Access Problem

We saw that traditional diets often fail, while effective GLP-1 medications were hard to access. We built AT Health to remove the barriers of cost and complexity, offering a seamless path to clinically proven treatment.

3

No Compromise on Quality

Our approach is simple: expert medical guidance, genuine UK-licensed medication, fast delivery, and real ongoing support — all at prices that won't break the bank.

We're not here to sell you a quick fix. We're here to help you achieve lasting results with safe, proven treatments and proper medical oversight.

How We're Different

Why Choose AT Health

01

Expert Medical Team

Every prescription is reviewed by UK-registered Pharmacist Independent Prescribers regulated by the General Pharmaceutical Council. Real clinicians who genuinely care about your safety and success — not algorithms, not chatbots.

02

Fast & Discreet Service

Next-day delivery on all medication. Temperature-controlled packaging. Completely confidential service. No awkward appointments, no waiting rooms, no judgement. Just fast, professional care delivered to your door.

03

Genuine UK Medication

We only supply authentic, UK-licensed medication from registered pharmacies. No grey-market alternatives, no counterfeit products. Every treatment is exactly what it says on the label, dispensed by Care Quality Pharmacy (GPhC: 9012435).
